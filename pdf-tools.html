Privacy Policy

PDF Tools: Merge, Split & More — com.honestsimpleapps.pdftools
Effective date: March 19, 2026

100% offline. Zero permissions. Your files never leave your device.

Overview

PDF Tools is a utility app that processes PDF files entirely on your device. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, how files are handled, and how advertising works.

How Your Files Are Handled

Your files never leave your device. All PDF processing (merge, split, compress, lock, unlock, rotate, image conversion) is performed locally on your phone using the PdfBox-Android library. No files are uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third-party API. The app works identically in airplane mode.

Files are accessed through Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF). When you select a file, Android grants the app temporary permission to read that specific file. The app cannot browse your files or access anything you haven't explicitly selected. Temporary file data used during processing is cleared immediately after the operation completes.

Permissions

PDF Tools requires zero runtime permissions. The Storage Access Framework provides file access through the system file picker without requiring READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, or MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE. The Android Photo Picker provides image access for the Images-to-PDF feature without any storage permission.

The only permission present in the app manifest is INTERNET, which is a "normal" Android permission (not a "dangerous" permission) and is required solely for loading advertisements via Google AdMob. If you purchase "Remove Ads," no network requests are made by the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores a minimal amount of data locally using Android DataStore (Preferences). This data never leaves your device.

  • Ad removal status: Whether you purchased the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase
  • Ad-free timer: Timestamp for when a rewarded ad-free period expires

No PDF files, file names, or operation history are stored by the app. Each operation is stateless -- once the result is saved, the app retains no record of what was processed.

Advertising

The free version of PDF Tools displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. This includes banner ads and interstitial ads shown after task completion. The AdMob SDK may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) for the purpose of serving and personalizing advertisements.

You can control ad personalization through your Android device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

The app also offers optional rewarded ads: you may choose to watch a short video advertisement to temporarily go ad-free for 20 minutes, or complete a brief survey to go ad-free for 40 minutes. These are entirely voluntary and user-initiated.

No ads are shown during PDF operations. Ads only appear at natural breaks after a task is completed.

For details on how Google handles data collected through AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

PDF Tools offers a single in-app purchase: "Remove Ads" for $1.99. This is a one-time, non-consumable purchase processed through Google Play Billing. After purchase, no ads are displayed and no ad-related network requests are made. The purchase restores automatically across reinstalls via your Google Play account.

We do not process payments directly. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains advertisements which may not be suitable for or directed at children.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — for processing in-app purchases. Privacy Notice

The app also uses PdfBox-Android (Apache 2.0 license) for PDF processing. This is a local library that runs entirely on your device and does not communicate with any server.

Data Security

All network communication (ad requests, billing verification) is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. PDF files are processed in memory and temporary storage only during the active operation. No file data is retained after the operation completes. The app's local preferences are stored in Android's app-private data directory, accessible only to the app itself.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete. The app stores no record of files you have processed. Local preferences can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through Android Settings.
