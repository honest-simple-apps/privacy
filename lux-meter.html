Privacy Policy

Lux Light Meter — com.honestsimpleapps.luxmeter
Effective date: March 19, 2026

Zero permissions. Zero tracking. Works offline.

Overview

Lux Light Meter is a utility app that reads the ambient light level from your device's built-in hardware light sensor. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, what it stores, and how advertising works.

Permissions

Lux Light Meter requires zero runtime permissions. The ambient light sensor (TYPE_LIGHT) is a system sensor that does not require any permission to access. The app does not request access to your camera, location, contacts, storage, microphone, or any other device capability.

The only permission present in the app manifest is INTERNET, which is a "normal" Android permission (not a "dangerous" permission) and is required solely for loading advertisements via Google AdMob. If you purchase "Remove Ads," no network requests are made by the app.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your device using Android DataStore (Preferences). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

  • Unit preference: Whether you selected lux or foot-candles
  • Ad removal status: Whether you purchased the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase
  • Ad-free timer: Timestamp for when a rewarded ad-free period expires

Measurement history (saved readings) is held in memory only during the current app session and is cleared when the app is closed. No measurement data is persisted to disk or transmitted anywhere.

Advertising

The free version of Lux Light Meter displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. The AdMob SDK may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) for the purpose of serving and personalizing advertisements.

You can control ad personalization through your Android device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

The app also offers optional rewarded ads: you may choose to watch a short video advertisement to temporarily hide the banner ad for 10 minutes, or complete a brief survey to hide it for 20 minutes. These are entirely voluntary and user-initiated.

For details on how Google handles data collected through AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Lux Light Meter offers a single in-app purchase: "Remove Ads" for $0.99. This is a one-time, non-consumable purchase processed through Google Play Billing. After purchase, no ads are displayed and no ad-related network requests are made. The purchase restores automatically across reinstalls via your Google Play account.

We do not process payments directly. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains advertisements which may not be suitable for or directed at children.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Data Security

All network communication (ad requests, billing verification) is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Local preferences are stored in Android's app-private data directory, accessible only to the app itself.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete. Local preferences can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through Android Settings.
