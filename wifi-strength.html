Effective date: March 20, 2026

We never access your actual location. The location permission is required by Android to read WiFi data only.

Overview

WiFi Signal Strength is a utility app that reads WiFi signal information from your device's Android WifiManager API. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, what it stores, and how advertising works.

Permissions

WiFi Signal Strength requests the following permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: Required by Android (since Android 8.1) to read WiFi network information such as SSID, signal strength, frequency, and channel. The app never accesses, records, or transmits your actual geographic location. This permission is used solely to call WifiManager APIs that Android restricts behind location access.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: Read WiFi connection details (signal strength, link speed, etc.).
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Check whether a network connection is available.
  • INTERNET: Required solely for loading advertisements via Google AdMob. If you purchase "Remove Ads," no network requests are made by the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your device using Android DataStore (Preferences). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

  • Theme preference: Light, Dark, or System
  • Ad removal status: Whether you purchased the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase
  • Ad-free timer: Timestamp for when a rewarded ad-free period expires

Background Work

WiFi Signal Strength runs zero background services. No WorkManager jobs, no alarms, no wake locks. The app only reads WiFi data while it is in the foreground and visible. It does zero work when not on screen.

Advertising

The free version of WiFi Signal Strength displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. This includes a banner ad at the bottom of the screen. The AdMob SDK may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) for the purpose of serving and personalizing advertisements.

You can control ad personalization through your Android device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

The app also offers optional rewarded ads: you may choose to watch a short video advertisement to temporarily hide the banner for 15 minutes, or complete a brief survey to hide it for 30 minutes. These are entirely voluntary and user-initiated.

For details on how Google handles data collected through AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

WiFi Signal Strength offers a single in-app purchase: "Remove Ads" for $0.99. This is a one-time, non-consumable purchase processed through Google Play Billing. After purchase, no ads are displayed and no ad-related network requests are made. The purchase restores automatically across reinstalls via your Google Play account.

We do not process payments directly. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains advertisements which may not be suitable for or directed at children.

Third-Party Services

Data Security

All network communication (ad requests, billing verification) is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Local preferences are stored in Android's app-private data directory, accessible only to the app itself.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete. Local preferences can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through Android Settings.
